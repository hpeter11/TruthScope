The third season of the American television series The Traitors was announced on February 7, 2024. The season premiered on January 9, 2025.


== Production ==
Similar to the previous seasons, primary filming takes place in Ardross Castle in the Scottish Highlands.


== Contestants ==

Similar to the second season, this season's cast was composed entirely of reality show participants and public figures. The 21 original players were revealed on 5 June 2024, with the surprise inclusions of Wes and Derrick being revealed in Episode 2.
